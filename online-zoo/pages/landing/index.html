Pay & feed

Pay with VISA card

On the payment page, you need to enter the details of your "Visa" card and make a transfer.
The operation will not take more than ~2 minutes.

Payment goes to the zoo

Payment goes to the account of the zoo
and volunteers with employees receive a notification about the donation.

The selected animal receives a treat

The staff takes the food you need for your chosen animal and delivers it to the animal.
With your help, the animal is happy!
